Federal Incentives

Federal Incentives

Because there is such a large social benefit to installing solar, the federal government currently pays 30% of every system sold through the Solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC)

Quick Facts
- The ITC currently deducts a 30 percent tax credit for solar systems on residential and commercial properties (Sec 48)
- A tax credit is a dollar-for-dollar reduction in income taxes that a person would otherwise pay.
